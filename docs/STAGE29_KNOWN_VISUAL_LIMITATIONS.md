# Stage 29 Known Visual Limitations

- Stage 29 still uses primitive-composed MVP proxy prefabs. It is a realistic readability pass, not final production art.
- Materials are simple URP/Standard-compatible colors and smoothness values; there are no authored textures, normals, decals, weathering masks, or final VFX.
- Terrain is demonstrated in the Stage 29 review scene and does not yet replace every live vertical-slice map surface.
- Fine-grid guidance is intentionally visible for validation; final art may need a subtler gameplay-tuned overlay.
- The screenshot capture is for validation and visual review, not marketing-quality capture.
- Stage 29 does not change economy spacing, AI pacing, combat balance, pathing authority, or placement validation rules.
